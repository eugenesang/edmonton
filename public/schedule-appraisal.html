How Our Rent-to-Own Program Works

Step 1 Initial Consultation

Our process begins with a no-obligation consultation. We’ll discuss your housing needs, financial situation, and long-term goals. This helps us determine if the Rent-to-Own program is the right fit for you.

Step 2 Home Search

Once you’re pre-qualified, we’ll help you find a home that meets your criteria in the Edmonton area. You choose the home, and we purchase it on your behalf.

Step 3 Lease Agreement

You enter into a lease agreement with Edmonton Real Estate Experts. This agreement outlines the terms of your tenancy and the purchase option. A portion of your monthly rent payments will be credited towards your future down payment.

Step 4 Live in Your Dream House

Move into your new home and start enjoying the benefits of homeownership while you build your financial stability. Use this period to improve your credit score and save for a down payment.

Step 5 Purchase the Home

At the end of the lease term, you have the option to purchase the home. The accumulated rent credits and any additional savings you’ve built up will be used towards your down payment.
